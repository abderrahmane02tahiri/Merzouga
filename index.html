Merzouga

Merzouga is a city in eastern Morocco, known for its rich history and cultural heritage. It is more than just a city, as it serves as a gateway to the Sahara Desert and the majestic Atlas Mountains.

Sahara Desert dunes

Merzouga is famous for its breathtaking Sahara Desert dunes, attracting tourists from all over the world for camel treks, sandboarding, and overnight camping under the stars.

Camelback

Exploring the vast and tranquil desert landscape on camelback, while watching the sunset over the dunes, is an unforgettable experience that can be enhanced by spending the night in a cozy desert camp and sharing stories with other travelers around a warm fire.

campfire

Visitors can enjoy traditional Moroccan meals and storytelling around a campfire, surrounded by the peaceful silence of the desert and the sound of camels resting nearby.

SERVICES

Saddle up and ride into the Sahara.

Motorbiking in the Desert

Experience the thrill of exploring the stunning Sahara desert of Merzoug on a motorbike tour, Journey through the dunes and explore the region's remote corners, taking in the stunning scenery and breathtaking sunsets.

Traditional Food Restaurants

Indulge in the delicious flavors of Moroccan cuisine at traditional food restaurants that offer a variety of local dishes such as tagines, couscous, and mint tea.

Traditional Berber tent accommodations

Immerse yourself in the unique culture of the Berber people by staying in a traditional tent in the desert. With its cozy atmosphere and rustic charm, it's the perfect way to experience the Sahara's unique beauty.

Sandboarding lessons and rentals

Want to try something new and exciting? Rent a sandboard and hit the dunes for an adrenaline-fueled adventure. With the help of an expert guide, you'll learn how to glide down the dunes like a pro.

Camel trekking

The desert town of Merzouga offers a myriad of exciting activities, from camel trekking to sandboarding down towering dunes, providing endless fun and adventure for visitors of all ages.

Sand dunes

Merzouga's beauty lies in its vast, rolling sand dunes that stretch as far as the eye can see, creating a serene and peaceful environment that is truly breathtaking.

Sunset
